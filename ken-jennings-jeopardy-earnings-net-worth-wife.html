Who Is Ken Jennings? Who Is His Wife?

Ken Jennings, age 45, is an American reality star, famed game contestant, and an author, mostly known for the game show Jeopardy. He is the only one contestant with 74 consecutive wins in Jeopardy till the date.

Ken was born on 23 May in 1974 in Edmonds, Washington, in the United States. He graduated from Brigham Young University, where he studied Computer Science and English.

Ken Jennings is a married man and has two children. He is married to Mindy Jennings, his college sweetheart from Brigham University. Ken and his wife Mindy shared their wedding vows on 16 September 2000 and are together ever since.

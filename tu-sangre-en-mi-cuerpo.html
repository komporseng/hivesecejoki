Overall Meaning

The lyrics of Angela Aguilar's song "Tu Sangre En Mi Cuerpo" center on the love between a father and his daughter. The father expresses his admiration and respect for his daughter, and his desire to always be there for her. He tells her that he will follow in her footsteps because she is his example. He also assures her that, even if they are separated by life's challenges, she will always exist in his heart like her blood in his body. The daughter responds by expressing her gratitude for her father's love, and her desire to always be his little girl.


The lyrics of this song encapsulate the deep and unconditional love that exists between a parent and child. The father is grateful to have his daughter in his life, and promises to always be there for her no matter what. He admires her and wants to be like her, showing that the love between a parent and child is not one-way, but reciprocal. The daughter, in turn, expresses her love for her father and her desire to always be his little girl. The theme of the song is universal, as it speaks to the deep love that exists between parents and children of all cultures and backgrounds.

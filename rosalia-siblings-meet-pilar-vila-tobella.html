Rosala siblings: Meet Pilar Vila Tobella

Rosalia Vila Tobella, who has her stage name as Rosalía is a widely known music figure based in Spain.

When it comes to the field of music she is an exception winning lots of followers due to her amazing songs.

Professionally she has been able to churn out lots of great pieces that are still relevant day in day out.

The 30-year-old musician was born on September 25, 1992, in Sant Cugat del Vallès, Spain.

DESPECHÁ (Motomami · 2022), Con Altura(Con Altura · 2019), SAOKO (Motomami · 2022), TKN(TKN · 2020)LA FAMA(Motomami · 2022), and BIZCOCHITO (Motomami · 2022) are some of the wonderful tunes that have catapulted her to the limelight.

Deep Sidhu Age & Early Life

Deep Sidhu was born in Sri Muktsar Sahib, Punjab, India, on February 15, 2022. He and his family resided in his hometown. He was 37 years old at the time of his death. He received his primary education at a private school in Sri Muktsar Sahib. He chose to become a lawyer after finishing his senior school studies. Career

Deep Sidhu was heavily affected by modeling and chose to pursue a career as a model. He was crowned Grasim Mr. Personality and Grasim Mr. Talented in 2006. He has worked with many well-known designers in Mumbai. Following that, he opted to pursue his legal career. At Sahara India Pariwar, he served as a legal counselor. Sidhu was a phenomenal performer. Deep also collaborated with Ekta Kapoor for almost three years.

Deep Sidhu’s Death

According to official authorities, he died in a car accident on the Kundli-Manesar highway in Kharkhoda, Haryana, India. He was traveling with his girlfriend, Reena Rai, who had suffered significant injuries but was still alive. Deep was just 37 years old at the time (at the time of his death). According to Sidhu’s family, the vehicle collision was not an accident but rather a premeditated murder. On February 17, 2022, the driver of a truck parked on the highway was detained.

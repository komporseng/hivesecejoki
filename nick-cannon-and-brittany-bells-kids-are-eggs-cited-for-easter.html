NICK CANNON AND BRITTANY BELL'S KIDS ARE 'EGGS-CITED' FOR EASTER

Nick Cannon and Brittany Bell’s kids are excited for Easter. Golden and Powerful visited the Easter Bunny earlier this week, which was quite the trip to remember.

Powerful enjoyed time spent with the flowers. The little one was captured on camera as she smelled the freshness that flowed from nature. “So adorable,” one fan said. “Fearless,” another social media follower wrote in reference to Powerful touching the flowers without concern about bees and other bugs. “I love it,” the fan said.

“My little peeps are Egg-cited for Easter,” Brittany shared of both her children. The celebrity mom also uploaded a picture of her kids embracing the Easter Bunny while sitting with the holiday character.

Brittany Bell is a devout Christian who recognizes Easter Sunday as Resurrection Day. In the Christian religion, Sunday is recognized as the day when Jesus Christ rose from the grave after being crucified the Friday before his glorious appearing.

Good Friday is recognized as a time of remembrance for the death of Jesus, which Christians believe serves as an opportunity to be saved from their misdeeds or “sins.” Christians often attend church services on Friday evening as well as Sunday morning where they celebrate the gift of salvation.

Brittany Bell recently showcased a portion of her baptismal ceremony, which is a ritual Christians undergo to celebrate what they believe to be a new life given by Jesus. “Your vows to God are never null and void,” Bell captioned on the video clip that featured the moment she went underwater and came up. “Remember who you are,” Brittany advised her supporters.

The beauty queen further celebrated her spiritual growth by saying, “No matter how many times you fall, when you finally get up and hold your stance. You will keep it. Remember who you are.”

Golden and Powerful are two out of seven children Nick Cannon fathers. The radio host is expecting his eighth child to arrive in the coming days.

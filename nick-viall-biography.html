Nick Viall Bio (Wiki)

Who is Nick Viall?

Nick Viall is an American Software Sales Executive, Model, and Reality Star.

Nick Viall’s best known as the star of season 21 of ABC’s The Bachelor.

He was also a contestant on the Bachelorette and Bachelor in Paradise.

Who are the parents of Nick Viall?

Nicholas Joseph “Nick” Viall was born on September 29, 1980, in Milwaukee, Wisconsin.

His parents are Christopher(father) and Mary Viall(mother).

He has four brothers: James Viall, Luke Viall, Samuel Viall, and Peter Viall.

He has six sisters: Jessica Viall, Sarah Viall, Teressa Viall, Bella Viall, and Olivia Viall.

Education History

Nick attended “Waukesha North High School” in 1999. Then, he went to the “University of Wisconsin”.

He graduated in 2004 with a Bachelor’s degree in accounting and supply chain management.

Nick Viall: Professional Life and Career

Nick Viall was competitive in track and field during High School and college.

Upon graduation, he signed on as an account executive for the cloud computing company, salesforce.

Viall also has some success as a Model. He’s represented by Wilhelmina International Inc.

Nick was the runner-up on the 10th season of the Bachelorette with Andi Dorfman.

He competed for Kaitlyn Bristowe’s heart in the 11th season but came up short, finishing in second place.

Nick was also a contestant in the third season of Bachelor in Paradise. He’s the star of season 21 of ABC’s The Bachelor.

Arrested Development star Alia Shawkat is pregnant with her first baby

Alia Shawkat has a bun in the oven!

The “Arrested Development” star was photographed shopping at Gelson’s Market in Los Angeles on Monday with her baby bump on display.

During the outing, Shawkat cut a casual figure in a partially unbuttoned white top and black bike shorts.

The pregnant actress, 34, accessorized with blue sunglasses and a white crossbody bag that rested above her budding belly.

The “Search Party” star has yet to address her pregnancy news or speak to her baby-to-be’s paternity, though she was spotted at a birth clinic with her arm wrapped around a mystery man earlier this month.

Page Six has reached out to her rep for comment.

While Shawkat has kept quiet about her love life over the years, she did come out as bisexual in a 2017 Out interview.

“I was a tomboy growing up, and I remember my mom asking me when I was 10, ‘Are you attracted to boys or girls?’ I said, ‘I don’t know,'” she recalled.

“I think balancing my male and female energies has been a big part of me growing as an actor.”

.

Two years later, the “Final Girls” star and her pal Brad Pitt were rumored to be in a relationship — which Page Six debunked.

Shawkat denied the speculation herself in June 2020, clarifying to Vulture that she and the actor, now 59, were “just friends” and “not dating.”

In January 2022, she told the New Yorker that Pitt had “no awareness” of the reports until she clued him in.

“He was, like, ‘I’m sorry. It happens. If you hang out with me, it happens,'” Shawkat explained, joking, “You get too close to the prom king, and all of a sudden everyone’s like, ‘Well, who is this bitch?’”

Meet Joe Millionaire's Andreea Natalia! Her Bio, Age, Height, Family, Job

Added: The new reality show on Fox, Joe Millionaire: For Richer or Poorer is a “fresh” take with a new twist on the original Joe Millionaire dating series that took the world by storm in 2003. The 6 January 2022-release features two single men — Steven McBee and Kurt Sowers — one of whom is secretly a millionaire.

Now, of course, there are contestants. The show features 18 eligible women looking to find love among these two distinct bachelors and Andreea Natalia is one of them.

Read along this ‘Andreea Natalia Bio’ and find out everything that you have been wondering about her.

Andreea Natalia On Joe Millionaire

In her Joe Millionaire girlfriend application, Andreea Natalia revealed one or two things about herself. She admitted that she is vegan and loves flowers and plants, that she is obsessed with traveling the world and culture. To this post on Instagram, Andreea’s costar from Joe Millionaire, Annie Jorgensen, was quick to drop in her comment that read “You’re so easy to love” and Andreea, hinting a friendship between them, fondly replied, “@anniejorgensen baby doll how could anyone not fall in love with YOU!”.

Yes, Annie, a former Miss Georgia, is exactly the one who fans believe to be Kurt’s frontrunner.

As for Andreea, she is on Joe Millionaire looking for a “real man”.

She knows love is not easy, but also has lived it enough to tell that it is always worth it. Last year, as Andreea spills, has been a crazy year full of so many adventures, friendships, growing pains, and love. This is why she decided to go on a reality TV show in search of her true love. “…and maybe give a couple of lap dances in the process”, she jested.

Andreea Natalia Age

Andreea Natalia was 31 years old when the world first knew about her Joe Millionaire stint.

Is Andreea Natalia On Instagram?

Yes, as told before, Andreea Natalia could be spotted on Instagram as of 25 January 2022. The account @andreeanataliaceo included 294 posts and some 6,179 followers.

You could find Andreea also on Twitter @andreeaceo and TikTok @dreeanatalia.

Andreea Natalia Height

Beautiful Andreea Natalia stands below 5’6” tall and her measurements were lately, reported as 37-25-39 (inches).

Andreea Natalia Job

As of December 2021, Andreea Natalia owned two coffee shops and built restaurant concepts. On her social media, she also introduced herself as an award-winning entrepreneur, restaurant developer, author, and Chef.

If you do not already know, Joe Millionaire is not her first time being on television. Even before that, she featured on HGTV besides appearing on The Boston Globe, The Rhode Show, and a few more.

Of all, Andrea is most proud of her Kaffeology Café that she incorporated in January 2018.

Speaking of her education, Andreea got her A.S. in Liberal Arts and Sciences/Liberal Studies at Bristol Community College (2009 – 2012). Then, only she went to Northeastern University to study BS in Biology/Biological Sciences (2012 – 2015).

What Do We Know About Andreea Natalia Family?

Andreea Natalia’s full name or official name is Andreea Natalia Marin.

She always wanted to open a cafe. Ever since a five-year-old, Andreea knew that cooking was what she wanted to do. However, she ended up heading to school to become a doctor. First, she thought she would open a cafe after she became a doctor. But then someday, she was like “Why don’t I do what I love and follow my passion and open my cafe?”. Enter her parents. From the look of things, they have been helping her out in her dream of opening a cafe at Newport.

When talking in an interview about her cafe, the outrageous milkshakes to coffee, and pastries that it offered, Andreea described how her milkshakes are inspired by many different things such as memories or her family. So, in June 2018, she for the first time revealed that she has a 13-year-old sister. She said her sister is the one who inspired the “fruity pebbles” milkshakes other than the salted caramel and s’mores ones.

While that is all that she had told us people about her family, Andreea also has some stories from her early life.

On Instagram, Andreea sadly recalled her traumas as a child and said “Our trauma shapes who we are. It shapes how we react to certain situations. How we deal with rejection. How we love. And how we live.”

Putting on online indeed an innocent picture of herself as young, Andreea went on to say “Often times I think about this sweet, innocent child. She was so eager to learn about the world, so fearless, so pure.” She said how she now wishes she could go back in time and hold her and tell her it will all be okay, wipe her tears away, ease her heartaches, and protect her from everything she was going to go through.

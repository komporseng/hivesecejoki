Biography

AJ Hawk was born in Kettering, OH on January 6, 1984. NFL linebacker who was drafted fifth overall by the Green Bay Packers in 2006. He was named to his first Pro Bowl in 2010 and he helped the Packers win Super Bowl XLV. After spending his first nine seasons with the Packers, he signed with the Cincinnati Bengals in 2015. He married the sister of NFL quarterback Brady Quinn. He starred as a two-time All-American linebacker at Ohio State University from 2002 to 2005 under head coach Jim Tressel .

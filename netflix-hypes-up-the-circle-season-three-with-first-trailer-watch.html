Netflix Hypes Up 'The Circle' Season Three With First Trailer - Watch!

Netflix is bringing back more of The Circle!

Season three of the hit reality series will return next week with four all new episodes, and a brand new cast. Michelle Buteau is back as the host.

If you’re unfamiliar, The Circle centers on a group of social media stars who flirt, befriend, piss off, and compete in challenges against each other on a unique social media platform to earn the ultimate cash prize as top influencer. With $100k on the line, will they be able to earn clout and figure out who is real and who is a catfish?

The cast for season three includes Nick, Ruksana, Calvin, Michelle, Kai, Daniel, Matthew, and sister duo Ava and Chanel.

The Circle premieres Wednesday, September 8, with new episodes dropping Wednesday, September 15, Wednesday, September 22, and the finale on Wednesday, September 29.

What rapper signed Nardo Wick?

Rapper Nardo Wick was born Horace Bernard Walls III in the United States on December 30, 2001.

He is most known for his 2021 single “Who Want Smoke?” which has a remix with fellow rappers G Herbo, Lil Durk, and 21 Savage and helped it to debut at number 17 on the US Billboard Hot 100 and get platinum certification from the RIAA. He is signed to Flawless Entertainment and RCA Records. Wick’s debut studio album, Who Is Nardo Wick?, was released the same year.

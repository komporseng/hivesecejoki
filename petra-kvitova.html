Who is Petra Kvitova:

Petra Kvitova is a famous Tennis Player. She was born on March 8, 1990 and her birthplace is Bilovec, Czech Republic. Petra is also well known as, Winner of the 2011 and 2014 Wimbledon Championships and 2011 WTA Tour Championships singles titles. She also reached the semi-finals of both the Australian Open and French Open in 2012.

Petra is originated from Czech Republic. As a child, she idolized Czech tennis star Martina Navratilova.

Pro Boxing Debut, Tragic Turn

Colon turned professional in 2012 with an outstanding 17-0-0 amateur record. But then in 2015, after a fight with Terrel Williams, he slumped into a brain injured, vegetative state, effectively ending his career.

This was a terrible blow for him, not only in his career but his personal life.

Legacy and Advocacy Efforts

Prichard Colón is remembered for much more than his wins and losses on the boxing mat. He also made great strides in advocating for spinal cord injury awareness and support.

He has taken the opportunity to raise awareness, offer support to victims of spinal cord injuries and campaign for more funding for research into spinal cord injuries, treatments and support services.

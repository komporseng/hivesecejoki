John Robert “Johnny” Hekker (born February 8, 1990) is an American football punter for the National Football League’s Carolina Panthers (NFL). He attended Oregon State and was signed as an undrafted free agent by the St. Louis Rams in 2012.
Hekker is a four-time Pro-Bowler and four-time First-Team All-Pro. He also holds the NFL record for the longest punt in Super Bowl history, a 65-yarder in Super Bowl LIII.
